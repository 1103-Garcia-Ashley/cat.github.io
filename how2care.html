1. Feed:

Feeding is the most important rule to follow! Cats need to be fed at least three times a day! Make sure to keep treats on hand;). You can figure out what your cat might like by trying different textures and seeing what your cat likes best.

2. Clean:

Cleaning is important for you, your home, and your cat. This entails of cleaning the litter box once a day (typically at the end of the day).

3. Comfort:

You can ensure your cats comfort by buying your cat a nice comfy bed!

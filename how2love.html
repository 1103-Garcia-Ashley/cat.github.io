1. Pet:

Petting a cat will show a cat love. This can be done by gently scratching their head and scratching their back and behind their ear.

2. Give Kisses:

You can achieve this by softly "kissing" your cat.

3. Give Treats:

Giving a cat treats will make your cat happy. This is the equivalent of bringing your friend their favorite snack.

1.Lint Roller:

Always have a lint roller on hand to get your cats hair off your clothes and lint roll where your cat has laid.

2. Allergies:

Keep allergy medicine on hand. Cat hair tends to make you sneeze and have itchy eyes.

3. Bedding

Wash your bedding (bed sheets, pillow case, lounge blankets) every other week to ensure that cat hair is cleaned off.
